Little bit about Bristol:

Bristol is the largest city in South West England and 8th largest in the UK. Bristol has long since been an important city. Until the industrial revolution, Bristol had been in the top 3 largest cities in the UK for centuries. It was then overtaken by the rapid growth of some of Birmingham, Manchester and Liverpool, though it still retained its importance as the dominant city in the region due to rapid population growth during the 19th century, spawning new industries and infrastructure projects.
